## Sequence Metrics & Optimization

### Target Metrics

| Metric | Target | Industry Avg |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Open Rate | 55% | 25-35% |
| Click Rate | 12% | 3-5% |
| Conversion Rate | 25% | 15-25% |
| Unsubscribe | <0.5% | 0.5-1% |

### Segmentation Strategy

**Segment A: Power Users (5+ workflows created)**
- Highlight Professional plan benefits
- Show advanced features they haven't tried
- Offer custom workflow bonus
- Goal: Convert to Professional/Enterprise

**Segment B: Moderate Users (2-4 workflows)**
- Show ROI of their specific workflows
- Recommend Professional plan
- Emphasize scalability
- Goal: Convert to Professional

**Segment C: Light Users (0-1 workflow)**
- Offer extended trial (7 more days)
- Provide free onboarding call
- Simplify plan options
- Goal: Activate first, then convert

### A/B Test Ideas

**Test 1: Day 13 Subject Line**
- A: "Last chance: Your trial expires tomorrow üòÆ"
- B: "Don't lose your progress (trial ends tomorrow)"
- C: "{{first_name}}, we need to talk about your trial"

**Test 2: Day 10 ROI Presentation**
- A: Calculator format (dynamic numbers)
- B: Case study format (similar user story)
- C: Plain text summary

**Test 3: Discount Offer**
- A: 20% off first 3 months
- B: First month free
- C: No discount, emphasize value instead
