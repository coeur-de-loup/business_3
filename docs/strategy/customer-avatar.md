## Copywriting Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
**Overall:** Professional but approachable, empathetic, authentic

**Do:**
- Use "you" and "your" (customer-focused)
- Acknowledge pain (empathy before solution)
- Be specific ("10 hours/week" not "save time")
- Sound human (not corporate)
- Show, don't tell (use examples)

**Don't:**
- Use jargon ("synergy," "paradigm shift")
- Make exaggerated claims ("world's best")
- Sound desperate ("please try our tool")
- Be generic ("we help agencies")
- Bury the lead (get to the point)
