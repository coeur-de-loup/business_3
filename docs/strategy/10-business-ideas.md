## Idea 1: RFP Response Validator

**Category:** Government/Enterprise B2B - Sales Automation

**Problem:** Companies spend 40+ hours responding to RFPs (Requests for Proposals) only to be disqualified for missing non-compliant answers or formatting errors.

**Solution:** Automated RFP compliance checker that validates responses against requirements documents before submission, flagging missing sections, format violations, and non-compliant answers.

**Target Customer:** B2B SaaS companies (50-500 employees) responding to 5+ RFPs/month, government contractors, and enterprise sales teams.

**Revenue Model:** Subscription - $149/mo for 10 RFPs, $299/mo for unlimited

**Time to Launch:** 4 weeks

**Simplicity Score:** 9/10
- Clear pain point with measurable ROI (saves $5,000+ per disqualified RFP)
- Simple tech: PDF/text parsing, rule-based validation engine, dashboard
- Solo-maintainable with <50 customers
- Viral in sales teams (shares across departments)
- Low churn once integrated into sales process

**Why It Works:**
- RFPs are high-stakes, high-stress events
- One failed RFP costs $50,000+ in wasted time
- Compliance rules are explicit (easy to code)
- Target market has budget and urgency
- Word-of-mouth spreads fast in sales circles

**Tech Stack:** Next.js + PostgreSQL + PDF.js for parsing
