# Content Writing Patterns

## Core Principles

- **Benefit over feature**: "Save 10 hours/week" not "Automated scheduling"
- **Specific over vague**: "Join 2,847 marketers" not "Join thousands"
- **Active over passive**: "Get started in 2 minutes" not "Getting started takes 2 minutes"
- **Short sentences**: Break up complexity. One idea per sentence.

## Email Sequences

### Welcome Sequence (5 emails)
1. Welcome + quick win
2. Your story + mission
3. Case study / proof
4. Feature highlight
5. Soft CTA

### Onboarding Sequence (7 emails)
1. Getting started
2. First milestone
3. Common mistakes
4. Power features
5. Success story
6. Check-in
7. Next level

## Headline Formulas

Always provide 3+ variations:

1. **How to [achieve result] without [common pain]**
2. **[Number] ways to [achieve result] in [timeframe]**
3. **The [adjective] way to [achieve result]**
4. **Stop [pain]. Start [benefit].**
5. **[Result] for [audience] who [situation]**
