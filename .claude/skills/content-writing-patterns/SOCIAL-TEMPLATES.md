## LinkedIn Formats

### Story Post
```
[Hook line - curiosity or result]

[Blank line]

6 months ago, I was [struggling situation].

Today, [current positive situation].

Here's what changed:

[3-5 lessons, each on new line]

If you're [in similar situation], try this:

[Specific actionable advice]

[CTA or question for engagement]
```

### Carousel Prompts
```
Slide 1: [Bold hook statement]
Slide 2: The problem...
Slide 3-7: [One point per slide]
Slide 8: Summary/takeaway
Slide 9: CTA + your info
```

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X

- Line breaks for readability

### LinkedIn
- First line is the hook
- Lots of white space
- Personal stories perform best
- Post 1x daily, engage 30min after

### Reddit
- Don't be promotional
- Provide genuine value first
- Follow subreddit rules exactly
- Build reputation over time
