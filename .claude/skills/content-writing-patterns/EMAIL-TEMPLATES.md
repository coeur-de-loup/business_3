# Email Sequence Templates

## Welcome Sequence

### Email 1: Welcome + Quick Win
```
Subject: Welcome! Here's your first win with [Product]

Hey [Name],

You're in!

Here's the fastest way to get value from [Product]:

[One specific action they can take in 2 minutes]

That's it. You'll [immediate benefit] right away.

Tomorrow, I'll share [teaser for next email].

— [Your name]

P.S. Hit reply if you have any questions. I read every email.
```

### Email 2: Story + Mission
```
Subject: Why I built [Product]

Hey [Name],

Let me tell you a quick story...

[2-3 paragraphs about the problem you experienced and why you built the solution]

That's why [Product] exists: to help [audience] [achieve result] without [pain].

Sound familiar?

— [Your name]
```

### Email 3: Case Study
```
Subject: How [Customer] achieved [specific result]

Hey [Name],

[Customer name] was struggling with [problem you solve].

Then they tried [Product].

Within [timeframe]:
- [Result 1]
- [Result 2]
- [Result 3]

Here's what they said:
"[Testimonial quote]"

Want similar results? [CTA]

— [Your name]
```

### Email 4: Feature Highlight
```
Subject: The feature our users love most

Hey [Name],

I asked 50 customers: "What's your favorite [Product] feature?"

The #1 answer? [Feature name]

Here's why it's so powerful:
[Explain benefit, not just how it works]

Have you tried it yet? [CTA to try feature]

— [Your name]
```

### Email 5: Soft CTA
```
Subject: Quick question

Hey [Name],

I noticed you signed up [X days] ago.

How's it going? Are you getting value from [Product]?

If you're stuck, hit reply — I'd love to help.

If you're ready to go further, [upgrade CTA].

Either way, I'm here.

— [Your name]
```

## Onboarding Sequence

### Email 1: Getting Started
```
Subject: Your [Product] setup guide (2 min read)

Hey [Name],

Let's get you set up for success.

Here are the 3 things to do first:

1. [Action] — [Why it matters]
2. [Action] — [Why it matters]
3. [Action] — [Why it matters]

Start here: [Link to first action]

Tomorrow, I'll show you how to [next milestone].

— [Your name]
```

### Subject Lines to Test

Always test 2-3 subject lines:

**Curiosity-based:**
- "The one thing most [audience] get wrong"
- "This changed everything for me"
- "Quick question about your [topic]"

**Benefit-based:**
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "[Result] in 3 simple steps"
- "Your [result] checklist"

**Personal:**
- "Quick update for you"
- "[Name], you there?"
- "Something I wanted to share"
