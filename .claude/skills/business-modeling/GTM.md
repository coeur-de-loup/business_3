# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Validation (Pre-launch)
- Build landing page with value prop
- Collect email waitlist
- Validate messaging with early signups
- Refine based on feedback

### Phase 2: Beta Launch
- Invite waitlist in cohorts
- Gather usage data and feedback
- Iterate on product rapidly
- Build testimonials and case studies

### Phase 3: Public Launch
- Product Hunt / Hacker News
- Press outreach
- Influencer partnerships
- Paid advertising tests

### Phase 4: Growth
- Double down on winning channels
- Build referral mechanisms
- Content marketing for SEO
- Community building

## Channel Strategy

### Free Channels
| Channel | Effort | Time to Results | Best For |
|---------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| SEO/Content | High | 3-6 months | Long-term traffic |
| Reddit/Forums | Medium | Weeks | Early validation |
| Twitter/X | Medium | Months | Building audience |
| Product Hunt | Low | Days | Launch spike |

### Paid Channels
| Channel | Min Budget | Best For |
|---------|------------|----------|
| Google Ads | $500/mo | Intent-based search |
| Meta Ads | $500/mo | Awareness, retargeting |
| LinkedIn | $1000/mo | B2B targeting |

## Launch Checklist

```
Pre-Launch:
[ ] Landing page live
[ ] Analytics configured
[ ] Email sequences ready
[ ] Social accounts created
[ ] Press kit prepared

Launch Day:
[ ] Product Hunt submission
[ ] Social announcements
[ ] Email to waitlist
[ ] Outreach to influencers
[ ] Monitor and respond to feedback

Post-Launch:
[ ] Analyze traffic sources
[ ] Follow up on signups
[ ] Collect testimonials
[ ] Plan next iteration
```
