# Discovering Opportunities

Delegates comprehensive market research to the market-researcher subagent.

## When to Use

- Exploring new market opportunities
- Validating business ideas
- Analyzing competitive landscape
- Researching trends and demand signals

## Expected Outputs

All research saved to `docs/research/`:
- `trends.md` - Market trend analysis
- `competitors.md` - Competitive landscape
- `opportunities.md` - Scored opportunity list
- `validation.md` - Demand validation evidence

## Quality Criteria

Research must include:
- Cited sources with URLs
- Quantified market data where available
- Opportunity scoring on: market size, competition, timing, feasibility
- Honest assessment of risks and challenges
